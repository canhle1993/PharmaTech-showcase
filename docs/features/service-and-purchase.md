# Service and Purchase Workflows

PharmaTech is not limited to product sales. It also supports service-related interactions that match a B2B machinery business model.

## Service Flows

- Consulting request
- Technical support request
- Equipment upgrade request
- Maintenance request

## Purchase Flows

- General purchase inquiry
- Customer intake
- Technical consulting
- URS development related workflow
- Contract signing related workflow

## Why This Matters

These modules make the platform more realistic for an industrial equipment company because customers may not always buy directly from a simple storefront. They often need:

- consultation first
- technical clarification
- custom request handling
- after-sales support

This broadens the system from a simple catalog website into a more complete business operations platform.
