# Client Features

PharmaTech provides a customer-facing website focused on product discovery, quotation, ordering, and company presentation.

## Product Discovery

- Browse pharmaceutical equipment by category
- View product listing pages
- Open product detail pages with gallery, description, and specifications
- See related products and structured machine information

## Quote and Purchase Journey

- Submit quote requests online
- Add items to cart
- Add products to wishlist
- Proceed through checkout
- See deposit calculation and payment-related status
- Upload payment proof and track order progress

## Profile and Account

- Register a new account
- Login and recover password
- Update personal information
- Maintain job preference and resume profile
- View order history and application status

## Informational Pages

- Home page with hero banner and featured sections
- About page introducing company profile
- Contact page with contact information and inquiry form
- Service pages for consulting, maintenance, support, and equipment upgrade

## Career Features

- Browse job postings
- Save jobs
- Submit applications
- Track recruitment progress after submission
